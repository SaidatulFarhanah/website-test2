ABOUT US

Pisang4U is a local business established in Malaysia in early 2022. Pisang4U's first branch is in Klang,Selangor and it is still operating today. Pisang4U is a business owned by Pisang4U Enterprise which is run by 4members who have important positions in the organization.

Logo of Pisang4U Enterprise

About Our Company

Prior to the establishment of Pisang4U Enterprise, there were several processes that needed to be faced to ensure that Pisang4U products met the taste and expectations of our customers. Pisang4U produces banana chips that have a variety of interesting flavors. The idea was sparked by one of the Pisang4U partnership members who also provided an idea to ensure the business runs smoothly.

Existing recipe have also been innovated by one of the members of this business partnership and have been in R&D for 3months prior to the official opening of Pisang4U. Among the flavors offered by Pisang4U to our customers are vanilla, chocolate, strawberry and green tea. The balanced taste of banana chips coated with various flavors makes Pisang4U a product that every Malaysian should try.
